Artificial intelligence:

Are we facing a future of robots running wild?, AUGUST 31, 2016

To advance artificial intelligence research, Professor Milind Tambe, Helen N. and Emmett H. Jones Professor at the USC Viterbi School of Engineering and Professor Eric Rice of the USC School of Social Work have joined forces to co-direct the Center on Artificial Intelligence for Society, or CAIS. At the newly announced center, one of the first such university-based institutes dedicated to studying AI as a force for good, researchers will leverage artificial intelligence to address myriad problems ranging from climate change to security to health to homelessness.
